  • How do I sign up?

    Go to our online form where you can enter your registration information.
    Remember that the registration desk is closed the night before the event.

  • What is the minimum fund-raising total I have to raise?

    There is no minimum sponsorship target.
    Any total will help in the cause. Just try and raise as much as you can.

  • Will I receive a shirt to wear at the race?

    Yes! We will be sending out a state of the art running vest or T-shirt to you in the post about a month before race day.
    Please remember to specify which vest or T-shirt size you would like when confirming your place.

  • When will my registration pack arrive?

    We will mail out your registration pack in the post within two weeks of your registration.
    If you have not received the pack within that time, please contact us.

  • What does it cost to register?

    $35 for the 5K/10K race.
